USA Power Index

2026 April: From 1937 to 2026, U.S. power has experienced three peaks: in 1957, 1999 and 2021. Before 2000, a peak occurred roughly every twenty years. After 2000, as global dynamics accelerated, peaks began appearing about every ten years. Following the current peak, a decade-long decline is expected, lasting until around 2030.

Japan Ambition Index

2026 April: Japan’s ambition index dropped sharply after World War II, then rose rapidly during the Korean War. In the 1970s, alongside a period of high-speed growth focused on the economy, Japanese ambition subsided somewhat. Before the Plaza Accord, Japan’s ambition reached its lowest point in peace time. Over the past decade, Japan’s ambition has increased rapidly and is expected to reach its historical peacetime peak around 2027.
